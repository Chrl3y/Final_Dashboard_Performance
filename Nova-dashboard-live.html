Total Portfolio
All-time cumulative principal ever disbursed. Not period-filtered. Grows only when new loans are disbursed.
SUM(principal_disbursed_derived) — status 300,500,600,602,700
PO
Portfolio Outstanding
Live principal balance owed across all active and in-arrears loans. Matches HelaPlus Balance Outstanding. Not period-filtered.
SUM(principal_outstanding_derived) — status 300,500,602
D
Total Disbursements
Principal disbursed in the selected period window. Period-filtered via disbursedon_date. Change chip shows % vs same prior period.
SUM(principal_disbursed_derived) WHERE disbursedon_date IN period
C
Collections
Total money received from borrowers in the period. Uses created_date (posting date) — matches HelaPlus Daily Collection Report exactly. Repayment type only (type_enum=2), excludes reversals.
SUM(amount) WHERE type=2 AND is_reversed=0 AND created_date IN period
E
Expected Collections
Scheduled installment amounts with duedate falling within the selected period, capped at today. "Today" = loans due today. "Week" = loans due Mon→today. "Month" = loans due 1st→today. No future dates included. Unpaid installments only (completed_derived=0).
SUM(principal+interest+fees) WHERE duedate IN period AND duedate ≤ CURDATE() AND completed=0
%
Collection Rate
How much of the scheduled obligation was actually collected. Subtitle shows live UGX breakdown. Target: ≥90% Excellent, 80–89% On Track, 50–79% Moderate, <50% Low. Applies per-officer in the leaderboard.
Collections ÷ Expected Collections × 100
#
Active Loans
Count of loans currently active (status=300, no overdue). Live snapshot — not period-filtered. Distinct from Active-in-Arrears (status=602) which are omitted from this count.
COUNT(loan_id) WHERE loan_status_id = 300
!
PAR > 30 Days
Portfolio At Risk — % of active portfolio with 30+ day overdue installment. Live snapshot. Red alert banner at ≥5%. Uses pre-calculated laa.principal_overdue_derived (Method A) — exact HelaPlus match. Never use overdue_since_date_derived for PAR.
SUM(laa.principal_overdue_derived) ÷ SUM(ml.principal_outstanding_derived) × 100
!!
PAR > 90 Days
UMRA regulatory provisioning trigger. Loans overdue by more than 90 days must be provisioned at 100% under Bank of Uganda/UMRA guidelines. Critical for regulatory compliance and financial reporting accuracy.
SUM(overdue_derived WHERE overdue_days > 90) ÷ SUM(outstanding) × 100
Ø
Avg Loan Size
Average principal disbursed per loan in the selected period. Tracks ticket-size trends — rising average with stable PAR suggests successful move up-market.
AVG(principal_disbursed_derived) WHERE disbursedon_date IN period
R
Customer Retention
% of borrowers who have taken 2+ loans (repeat-borrower rate). All-time — not period-filtered. High rate = satisfaction & loyalty. Low rate may signal repayment issues or competition.
COUNT(clients with ≥2 loans) ÷ COUNT(all clients) × 100
!
Overdue Amount
Principal of unpaid installments whose duedate falls within the selected period and is before today. Period-filtered and branch-filtered. Shows orange/red per officer in the leaderboard. Different from PAR — this is period-specific missed payments, not the cumulative risk rate.
SUM(GREATEST(principal_amount − completed − writtenoff, 0)) WHERE duedate IN period AND duedate < CURDATE() AND completed=0
⏱
Period Filters
Today: transactions/dues = today only.
Week: Mon→today.
Month: 1st→today.
Qtr/Year: quarter/year start→today.
All: everything up to today.
PAR, Active Loans, and Portfolio metrics are always live — not period-filtered.
m_loan_transaction.created_date | m_loan_repayment_schedule.duedate | m_loan.disbursedon_date
